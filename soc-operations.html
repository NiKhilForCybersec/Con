SOC Metrics

Key performance indicators for SOC effectiveness:

Metric Description Target Why It Matters
MTTD Mean Time to Detect Minutes-hours How quickly threats are identified
MTTR Mean Time to Respond Hours for critical How quickly threats are contained
MTTA Mean Time to Acknowledge <15 min critical Queue management effectiveness
False Positive Rate FP / Total Alerts <30% Detection quality
Alert Volume Alerts per analyst per day 20-40 actionable Workload management
Escalation Rate % escalated to Tier 2+ 15-25% Tier 1 effectiveness
Dwell Time Time threat is undetected Days (industry: months) Overall security posture

| where Status != "New"
| extend MTTA = datetime_diff('minute', FirstModifiedTime, CreatedTime)
| summarize 
    AvgMTTA = avg(MTTA),
    MedianMTTA = percentile(MTTA, 50),
    P95MTTA = percentile(MTTA, 95)

| where TimeGenerated > ago(30d)
| where Status == "Closed"

    Total = count(),
    FalsePositive = countif(Classification == "FalsePositive"),
    TruePositive = countif(Classification == "TruePositive"),
    BenignPositive = countif(Classification == "BenignPositive")

| order by Total desc

Enrichment for Triage

Effective triage requires context. Key enrichment sources:

  • Asset inventory: Is this a critical server? Executive workstation?
  • User context: VIP? Privileged? Service account?
  • Threat intel: Known malicious IP/hash? Related to active campaign?
  • Historical context: First time this user/host triggered this alert?
  • Related alerts: Part of a larger attack chain?
